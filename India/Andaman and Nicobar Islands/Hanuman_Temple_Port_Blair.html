Hanuman Temple Port Blair

Sri Veer Hanuman Temple is located at Port Blair city belongs to South Andaman district in Andaman and Nicobar Islands union territory of India. Andaman and Nicobar Islands one of the seven union territories of India are a group of islands at the juncture of Bay of Bengal and Andaman Sea. Port Blair is the capital of Andaman and Nicobar Islands. This famous Veer Hanuman Temple is situated on MG Road Aberdeen Bazar in the city.
