ISKCON Temple Delhi New Delhi

ISKCON Temple Delhi New Delhi Sri Sri Radha Parthasarathi Mandir generally known as the ISKCON Delhi temple is a well known Vaishnav temple of Lord Krishnaand Radharani in the form of Radha Parthasarathi. Opened in 1998 it is located at Hare Krishna Hills near Nehru Place in the East of Kailash area of New Delhi India. ISKCON Temple designed and built by Achyut Kanvinde who in 1993 agreed to accept a pro-bono commission to build this temple complex for the followers of Srila Prabhupada is one of the largest temple complexes in India. It comprises numerous rooms for priests and for service renders. It has many halls that are used for its administration purposes and various seminars. It is divided into four broad sections.
