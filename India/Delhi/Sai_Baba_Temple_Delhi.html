Sai Baba Temple Delhi

Located on Lodhi Road it is one of the oldest Sai Baba temple in Delhi. The temple is simple yet majestic and if people are to be believed then many testimonies regarding feeling the divine presence of Baba have been recorded since the time the temple was built. The temple has a big idol of Sai Baba that sits in the main hall where devotees offer chadar and flowers. A sizable population throngs the shrine on Thursdays each week here.
