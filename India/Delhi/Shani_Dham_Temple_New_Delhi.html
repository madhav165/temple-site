Shani Dham Temple New Delhi Shani Dham Temple is located in the Indian capital of Delhi and contains the world s tallest statue of Shani.In the last few years i.e. on May 31 2003 the largest Shani Idol was unveiled by Anant Shree Vibhushit Jagat Guru Shankracharya Swami Madhavasharam Ji Maharaj. Since then it has become an attraction for the devotees of Lord Shani.
