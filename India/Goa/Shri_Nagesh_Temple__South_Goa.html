Shri Nagesh Temple South Goa

Another important and significant temple among the temples in Goa is the Nagesh Temple. Naguesh Temple is famous for its strong stable background. It is the only temple in Goa that was not rebuilt ever and was just renovated. You would not want to miss on the chance to see the beautiful reflections of the images that are created in the pool of the temple.
