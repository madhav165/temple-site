Parshurama Temple South Goa

The temple of Lord Parshurama is located at Painguinim in the area of Canacona Goa. It is dedicated to Lord Parshurama the sixth avatar of Lord Vishnu. The temple is located around the serene surroundings of forest grove. In the courtyard of the temple there s also a representation of Kshetrapala . The temple has traditional carved wooden pillars and traditional pyramid shaped Shikara. Talem Poinguinim Goa 403702
