Shri Damodar Temple South Goa

Situated on the banks of the Kushawati River Shri Damodar Temple is the home to the deity of Lord Shiva s incarnation as Lord Damodar. A large number of devotees believe that Kushavati River can heal the body ills with its healing power. According to the temple rituals there is a Palanquin carried out where the ideal of the Lord Damodar is held around the temple premises. During the procession it takes around fourteen halts to just stand for a while and sing bhajans. Do your next trip to goa some justice and visit this one of the famous temples in Goa to witness the colours and songs of peace. Located near Zambaulim village 22 km south of Margao. From Quepem take the right turn 5 km ahead to Tilamol-Rivona-Curtorim road leading to Zambaulim. Fatorda Margao Goa 403602
