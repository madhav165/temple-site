Shri Kamakshi Temple South Goa

Another spectacular creation the temple of Shri Kamakshi stands in amazing surroundings which adds to its beauty and charm. A divine ambiance is all what a pilgrim needs when he comes this far. Unlike other famous temples in Goa Kamakshi Temple is the one which abodes a water tank in its sanctum. The splendid beauty and the divinity of the place attracts tourists from all over Goa.
