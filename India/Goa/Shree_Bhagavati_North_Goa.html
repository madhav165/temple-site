Shree Bhagavati North Goa

28 kms from Panaji in Pernem Taluka the temple is said to be more than 500 years old. Made of black stone two life size images of elephants in a standing position at the entrance welcomes visitors. Dussehra is celebrated with gaiety when over 25 thousand devotees assemble at the temple. At a distance of 27 km from Panjim Kadamba Bus Stand 54 km from Vasco Da Gama Railway Station and 15 km from Mapusa Shri Bhagwati Temple is situated inNorth Goa.
