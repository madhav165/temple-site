Shri Ramnath Temple South Goa

Historically Ramnath is considered to be one of the forms of Lord Shiva to whom Lord Ram paid tributes and prayed before crossing the waters to enter Sri Lanka. Therefore Shri Ramnath Temple holds a huge amount of importance in the Hindu culture and this is one of the famous Hindu temples in Goa. What you just cannot miss while in this temple is the deepstambh and the idols of other Gods besides Lord Ramnath. It s located in Ramnathim Bandivede at some 30 kms from Panjim. You need to take NH 17B and turn left at Borim to reach here. It ll take you around 45 minutes to reach here from Panjim.
