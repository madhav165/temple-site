Mahadeva Temple North Goa

Declared as a protected monument Mahadeva Temple is historic enough to fall in the category of the old temples in Goa. Dedicated to Lord Shiva is a unique self on the architectural basis. A pillared entrance hall made up of the finest basalt stones and also bears a three-stepped entrance to worship the linga. The Mahadev Temple in Goa attracts devotees and nature lovers as well throughout the year. Near Bhagwan Mahaveer Wildlife Sanctury Sanguem Surla Goa 403406
