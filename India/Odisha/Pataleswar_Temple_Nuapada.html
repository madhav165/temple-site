Pataleswar Temple Nuapada

Budhikomna houses the famous temple of Pataleswar made of bricks in pancharatha style unique in the state. A connoisseur of art and architecture finds immense pleasure in observing the different facets of this place. It is also a popular pilgrim centre in the locality but due to lack of minimum infrastructure facility tourist as well as pilgrims suffer a lot. Raipur Chhatishgarh is the nearest airport which is 162 KMs For all train Khariar Road is suitable station to board and it is 48 KMs from Khariar Road.
