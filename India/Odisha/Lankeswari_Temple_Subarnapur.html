Lankeswari Temple Subarnapur

Lankeswari Temple is located inside the river Mahanadi at Sonepur town Subarnapur district Odisha . Once upon a time Sonepur region was known as Paschime Lanka. Goddess Lankeswari was the presiding deity of Paschima Lanka. Now Maa Lankeswari is worshiped by the Kaivarta fisherman community of Sonepur and people of Subarnapur district. One can see this temple while traveling on the road connecting Ulunda Dharmasala Sonepur and Birmaharajpur. Raipur Bhubaneswar are the nearest Air-Port. Balangir is the nearest railway station. Connected to Bhubaneswar and Raipur . NH-57 crosses across the district. Sonepur Odisha 767017
