Maa Ambika Temple Mayurbhanj

The Ambika temple Baripada is an ancient temple and the Bijesthali of the presiding deity. People of Baripada strongly believe her as The Living Goddess who as a mother showers her blessings to all the mankind. Peoples come to worship here from different states like Jharkhand and West Bengal. Many festivals namely Durga puja Basanti Puja Pna Sankranti Makar Sankranti etc. are observed here.
