Dumriput Temple Koraput

A village stands between Koraput and Sunabeda by the side of NH-26 the famous Sri Ram Temple situated in the locality is widely known for the highest kneeling Hanuman Statue in Odisha. Shree Ram Navami festival is popularly celebrated every year which attracts a large number of devotees. It is 12 Kms away from Koraput. Dumuriput is 12 Kms away from Koraput.
