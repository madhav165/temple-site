Chandaneswar Temple Balasore

Chandaneswar is an acclaimed religious and cultural site of North Odisha which is famous for Lord Shiva temple. Chadak Mela is an important festival of this holy shrine. During Chadak Mela month of April and Shivarati lakhs of pilgrims devotees of Odisha West Bengal Bihar and other places of India visit place to pay obeisance to Lord Chandaneswar. The Nearest Air Port is Bhubaneswar which is 302 km. away. The nearest Railway Station is Jaleswar 40km and Digha 6km . Chandaneswar is connected with a good motorable road from Balasore 88 Kms. and Digha 6 Kms. .
