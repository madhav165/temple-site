Pahadi Mandir Jharsuguda

Pahadi Mandir is located at a distance of 3km from Jharsuguda Railway Station 3.5 km from bus stand on a small hilltop.Lord Shiva is worshipped at the temple. A beautiful view of Jharsuguda town can be seen from the hilltop. The nearest airport is at Jharsuguda. The nearest railway station is Jharsuguda Railway Station. Through local conveyance.
