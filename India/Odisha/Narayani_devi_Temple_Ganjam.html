Narayani devi Temple Ganjam

The scenic splendor of Narayani on the lap of the celebrated Vallery Mountain is a famous place in the eastern ghats. Its romantic setting with a perennial spring and green mango groves attracts a good number of visitors every day. The holy shrine of Goddess Narayani Durga with six hands is a great attraction for the devotees and picnickers. Nearest Airport is Bhubaneswar Nearest Railway Station is Balugaon On Road it is 15 k.m. from Balugaon
