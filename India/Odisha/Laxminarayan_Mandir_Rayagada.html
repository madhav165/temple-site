Laxminarayan Mandir Rayagada

Famous Laxminarayan Temple situated at IMFA Factory Therubali. The deities Laxminarayan Hanuman Lord Jagannath Balabhadra and Subhadra and Lord Siva also worshiped by thousands devotees. Nearest air port is at Visakhapatnam Andhra Pradesh 200 KMs away which is well connected with New Delhi Kolkata Chennai Mumbai Nagpur by going flights. Well connected from other parts of India like Delhi Chennai Allepy Bokaro Raipur Gujrat Nagpur Kolkata Hyderbad Bilaspur and Bhubaneswar. Convenient road transport facilities available as NH-43 is passing through Koraput i.e. 109 KMs from Rayagada. Direct Express bus services are available from Vishakhapatnam Vizianagarm Bhubaneswar Cuttack Puri Jeypore Koraput Malkangiri Phulbani etc. Therubali Gujalpadu Odisha 765018
