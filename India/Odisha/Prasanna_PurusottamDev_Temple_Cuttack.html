Prasanna PurusottamDev Temple Cuttack

Sri SriPrasannaPurusottamDev Temple of Tigiria is the second highest temple of Odisha and one among the oldest temple. The site of the temple is memorizing. The temple was built in 1787 by Sri Sankarshan Mandhata completed by Sri Banamali Champatsingh Mohapatra the then king of the Tigiria State.
