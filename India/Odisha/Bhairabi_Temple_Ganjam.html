Bhairabi Temple Ganjam

The place is named after the presiding deity Goddess Bhairabi at remote village Mantridi. Bhairabi now a days has become an important Shakti Pitha. Tourists from all most all parts of Odisha as well as northern Andhra Pradesh visit this holy shrine everyday. Nearest Airport is Bhubaneswar Nearest Railway Station is Berhampur By Road it is 18 k.m. away from Berhampur Mantridi Odisha 761008
