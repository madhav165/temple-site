It is famous as Vaishnab shirine. Also know as Remuna Gupta Vrindaban.It is surounded by another famous Khirachora Gopinatha Temple is situated in Remuna. It is a little town located 9 km east of Balasore about halfway between Howrah and Bhubaneswar in Orissa . The name Remuna is resulting from the word Ramaniya which means very good looking.The nearest train junction to Remuna is at Balasore which is the first major town in Odisha coming from Calcutta . One can get a night train from Calcutta that stops in Balasore in the early morning hours. From there one can get a taxi or bus to Remuna. If one has arrived Remuna in the morning one can see the Deities and then catch a night train to Puri . The Nearest Air Port is Bhubaneswar which is 220 km. away from Remuna The nearest Railway Station is Balasore Railway Station which is 10 km away from Remuna. Remuna is well connected by road. A number of Public Transport i.e. Buses Taxi Autorikswa are running from Balasore Town to Remuna daily. It is 10 KM away from Balasore Town.
