Bada Ganesh Ka Mandir Ujjain

Dedicated to Lord Ganesha Bada Ganesh Ka Mandir is a popular religious tourist attraction in Ujjain and counted among the best temples in Madhya Pradesh. Hundreds of people from different parts of the country throng this temple every year. The most fascinating aspect of this temple is that apart from the spot Ganesh Ka Mandir also serves as a place of learning astrology and Sanskrit. This is another reason why it draws in a large number of visitors. Perfect for those who are not just spiritually inclined but also wants to learn the ancient language Sanskrit and keen to practice astrology a visit to this Madhya Pradesh Temple is a must. Bada Ganesh Ka Mandir Mahakaleshwar Temple Ujjain Madhya Pradesh.
