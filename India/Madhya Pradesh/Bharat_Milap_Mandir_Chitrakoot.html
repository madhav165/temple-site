Bharat Milap Mandir Chitrakoot

One of the most interesting temples in Chitrakoot is the Bharat Milap Mandir which is located along the Parikrama of Kamadgiri. The place where the temple is built is believed to the place where Lord Rama met with his brothers during his period of exile in the Ramayana. It is accepted that Lord Rama s brother Bharat with his army and royal family stayed outdoors here. Some of the astounding sights which you can look forward to at Bharat Milap Mandir is the footprints of Lord Rama and his family. Kamta Mohalla Sitapur Chitrakoot Uttar Pradesh 210204
