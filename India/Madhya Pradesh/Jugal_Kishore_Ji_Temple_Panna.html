Jugal Kishore Ji Temple Panna

Jugal Kishore Ji Temple was built by Raja Hindupat Singh the fourth Bundela King of Panna during his reign from 1758 to 1778. According to the legends the idol placed in the garbha griha of this temple has been brought from Brindavana through Orcha. The ornaments and attire of the lord reflect the Bundelkhandi style. The temple has all the architectural features of Bundela temples including a nat mandapa bhog mandapa and a pradakshna path.
