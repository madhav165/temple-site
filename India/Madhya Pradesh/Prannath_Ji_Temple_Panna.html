Prannath Ji Temple Panna

Mahamati Prannathji temple is an important pilgrimage of Pranamis and attracts number of devotees during Sharada Purnima. It is believed that Mahamati Prannathji lived at the site for 11 years after which he took samadhi inside one of the domes of this temple. The temple was built in 1692 and has Muslim and Hindu architectural styles in its domes and lotus formations. The temple is divided into six parts namely Shri Gummatji Shri Bangalaji Shri Sadguru Mandir Shri Baijurajji Mandir Shri Chopada Mandir and Shri Khijada Mandir. The prime attraction of this pilgrimage site is Shri Gummatji which is a circular building with nine marble domes. . Eight of these domes represent the eight directions and central dome has a divine golden Kalasha on which a hand Paw is situated which represents 5 powers of Mahamati. Apart from this Kamani Darwaza is a famous temple gate which is constructed by using silver metal.
