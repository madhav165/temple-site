Bhairav Parvat Ujjain

Perched on the top of Bhairav Hills by the banks of River Shipra Bhairav Parvat is a famous temple in Madhya Pradesh. Also called Gadkalika by the locals the temple boasts of unique architecture with gorgeous stone inscriptions. The Shakti Peeth is believed to be the place where Ma Sati s elbow fell after Lord Shiva cut the burnt corpse by Sudarshan Chakra into 52 pieces. The goddess is revered as Avanti in this temple and always adorned with red which is considered as a sacred color for married women. 096116 64411
