Navagraha Mandir Ujjain

Situated on the Triveni Ghat of the Shipra the temple is located away from the old site of Ujjaini town. It is dedicated to the nine planets attracts large crowds on new moon days falling on Saturdays. Its religious importance has increased in recent years though there is no known reference to it in the ancient texts. The Nearest Airport Devi Ahilyabai Holkar Airport Indore 53 km . There are regular flights from Delhi Mumbai Pune Jaipur Hyderabad and Bhopal. Ujjain is a railway station on the Western Railway zone. It s code is UJN. Trains are available for many big cities.
