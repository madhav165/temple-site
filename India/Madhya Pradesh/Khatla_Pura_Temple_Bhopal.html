Khatla Pura Temple Bhopal

This is one of the oldest temples in the country dedicated to Lord Rama. It was built in the 19th century over 150 years ago. The temple also displays idols of Goddess Sita Lord Laxman etc. The Do-Gyaras festival is held in this temple which is the focal point of Khalapura s attraction.
