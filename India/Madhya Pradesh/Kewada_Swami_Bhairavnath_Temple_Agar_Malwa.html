Kewada Swami Bhairavnath Temple Agar Malwa

There is Kewada Swami Bhairavnath Temple near the official Animal Breeding Farm Agar on the famous Motasagar Talab of Agar the idol here is unique and lies in the trunk of a huge tree. It is believed that Bhairav Maharaj used to disturb people in the form of hair the devotees had stopped through the mechanism by planting the tree since then the tree is planted for the purpose of this tree. This is Kevada flower garden. It is believed that this idol was established in 1424 by Lord Raghau Dev of Gujarat. Here travelers come from distant places.
