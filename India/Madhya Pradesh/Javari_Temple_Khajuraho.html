Javari Temple Khajuraho

Famous for its wonderful architecture Javari Temple is one of the notable shrines in the Eastern Group of Khajuraho temples. It is a belief that this temple is named after Javari who has owned the land on which the temple is constructed. The beautiful Temple is known for its immaculate design and carvings that reflects the architectural brilliance of a bygone era. With various sculptures adorning its walls Javari Temple is one of the finest examples of ancient architecture. Eastern Group of Temples Sevagram Khajuraho Madhya Pradesh 471606
