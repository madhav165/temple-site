Raghunath Singha Dev Bankura

Raghunath Malla Dev also known as Raghunath Singha Dev was the fifty-first king of the Mallabhum now in the Indian state of West Bengal . He ruled from 1626 to 1656 CE.Because Kalaram son of Dhari Hambir Malla Dev was unfit to become a king due to his physical inability his mother who was Dhari Hambir s wife enthroned her husband s young brother Raghunath Malla Dev who later got the title of Singha
