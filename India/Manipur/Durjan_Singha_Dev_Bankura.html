Durjan Singha Dev Bankura

Durjan Singha Dev was the fifty-third king of the Mallabhum. He ruled from 1682 to 1702. Durjan Singha Dev 1682 1702 the son of Bir Singha Dev was opposite to his father. He was not at all cruel on the contrary he had a very gentle polite character. He was logical kind hearted and also pious. He established Madan Mohan temple. During his period several Portuguese French British traders came to this place. He was on the one hand a Vaishnava devotee and on the other hand a great warrior.
