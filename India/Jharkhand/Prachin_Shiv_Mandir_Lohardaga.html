Prachin Shiv Mandir Lohardaga

Prachin Shiv mandir Khakparta is one of the most popular visitor spots in Lohardaga district. This temple is devoted to Lord Shiva. This is located at atop a small hill in the Khakparta village of Lohardaga Sadar Block. It is famous for its scenic beauty and tranquil ambiance. This Temple is 8 km apart distance from Lohardaga district headquarters. You can visit this famous mythological site from private vehicles via Haramu Bhakso. The nearest airport to Lohardaga is Ranchi which is located approximately 70 km away from here. Lohardaga railway station is connected to the Tori station of the Latehar district and state capital Ranchi The capital Ranchi which is located approximately 70 km away from here.
