Jayda Temple Kharsawan

An Ancient Temple of Lord Shiva one of the pilgrimages of Jharkhand. Situated on the way to Ranchi-Tata and Shore of Swarnarekha river. A Fair is held every year on the eve of Makar Sankranti Tusu Festival . You can reach Jayda temple from Kolkata Airport or Ranchi Airport By train. Nearest railway station is chandil.
