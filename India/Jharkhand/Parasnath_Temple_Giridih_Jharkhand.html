Parasnath Temple Giridih Jharkhand

Shikharji is called as the Parasnath Temple and is located on the Parasnath Hills the highest mountain range of Parasnath Range. This temple is sacred for Jains. It is believed to be the place where twenty of the twenty-four Jain tirthankaras along with many other monks attained Moksha according to Nirvana Kanda and other texts. Sanskrit inscriptions at the foot of the image date to 1678. The nearest railway station is Parasnath Railway Station which is situated at Isri bazar Dumri. Several long distance trains stop at this station. By Airway Nearest airport is Kazi Nazrul Islam Airport Durgapur RDP West Bengal and 1-hour drive from the airport.
