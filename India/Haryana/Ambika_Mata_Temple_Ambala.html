Ambika Mata Temple Ambala

It is not only the oldest shrines but also the origin of the place. The name of Ambala is on the name of this goddess Amba. Dedicated to Goddess Amba Ambika and Ambalika this ancient Hindu shrine was constructed before the British era. One of the attractions of the temple is paintings on the upper walls and ceilings which have faded over the years. It is situated close to the old civil hospital which can be approached by road from any part of the city. Nearest Airport is Chandigarh International Airport.Which is 46 Kms away from Ambala City. Nearest railway stations are Ambala city 3 Kms and Ambala Cantt 8 Kms . Ambala is on Chandigarh to Delhi Highway which is NH1. There is a regular bus service from Ambala to Chandigarh and vice versa.
