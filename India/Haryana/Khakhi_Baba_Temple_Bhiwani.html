Khakhi Baba Temple Bhiwani

Khakhi Baba Mandir is dedicated to Saint Khakhi Baba a renowned sage of Bhiwani and passionate devotee of Lord Rama. He built temple of Lord at this place and devoted many years of his life in this temple. Hence the name of this temple is given after him Khakhi Baba Mandir. There are also showcased the idols of Lord Hanuman and Lord Shiva in the vicinity of Lord Rama idol. The nearest airport is Indira Gandhi International airport is at a and distance of 124 kms.
