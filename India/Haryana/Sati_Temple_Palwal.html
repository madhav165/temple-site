Sati Temple Palwal

In Hodal city there is a temple and a pond named Sati. Every year in the month of January there is a Mela organised and womenfolk pray Devi Sati and men take part in wrestling and other rural sports. The nearest airport is at IGI Airport Delhi 90 kms. from Hodal. The nearest railway station is Hodal on Delhi-Agra rail network. Hodal is on national highway no. 2 just 90km from Delhi.
