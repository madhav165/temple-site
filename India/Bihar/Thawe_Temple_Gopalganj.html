Thawe Temple Gopalganj

In Thawe block there is an old temple dedicated to Goddess Durga popularly known as Thawe wali Mata . It is believed that Thawe wali Mata fulfill the wishes of her devotees. This temple is also famous for a peculiar tree the botanical family of which has not yet been identified. The tree has grown up like the cross. Various legends are prevalent in connection with the idol and the tree. A big fair is annually held here in the month of Chaitra March -April . Sabeya is the nearest airport. After landing here anyone can visit here by road. Thawe is a junction station of Siwan-Gorakhpur loop line which is nearby the temple. From District HQ there is a direct road from Arar More.
