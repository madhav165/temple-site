Ashokdham Temple Lakhisarai

Ashokdham Mandir also known as Indradamneshwar Mahadev Mandir is located in Lakhisarai district Bihar. It is a temple complex having Indradamneshwar Mahadev Mandir in the center dedicated to the primary deity lord Shiva surrounded by three more temples dedicated to Goddess Parvati mount of the Shiva Nandi and Goddess Durga.
