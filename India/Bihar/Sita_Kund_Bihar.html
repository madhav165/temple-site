Sita Kund Bihar The Punaura Dham janki mandir is a Hindu pilgrimage site in Sitamarhi district in the Indian state of Bihar which has an ancient Hindu temple. It is situated 5 km west of Sitamarhi town and a popular visitor s attraction.
