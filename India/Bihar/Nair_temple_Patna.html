Nair temple Patna

The Nair temple is a ruined pillared temple at the location of Nair on the road between Patna and Gaya west of Dharawat. It is about 20 miles from Gaya. The temple consists of three rows of monolithic pillars ten pillars in each row. In the front of the temple there is a further row of four pillars.
