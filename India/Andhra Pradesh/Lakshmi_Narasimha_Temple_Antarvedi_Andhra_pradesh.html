Lakshmi Narasimha Temple Antarvedi Andhra pradesh There are three ways that one can travel to Antarvedi. Steam launches are available. Alternatively one can ride on the ferry upon reaching Sakhinetipalli and from there start for Antarvedi by road. The third option is to cross the Chinchinada using the newly built bridge and travel the rest of the way by road. Official Address
