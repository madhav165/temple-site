Paritala Anjaneya Temple Krishna

Paritala Anjaneya Temple is a temple residing statue of Lord Hanuman. The statue is the tallest one dedicated to Lord Hanuman in the world.It is located in the village of Paritala on NH-9 approximately 30 km from the city of Vijayawada in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. The statue was installed in the year 2003 and stands 135 feet 41 metres tall. The statue is taller than Christ the Redeemer statue in Rio de Janeiro Brazil 38 m including 8 m pedestal and attracts many devotees both foreign and domestic. The tallest Lord Hanuman statue outside India is at Carapichaima Trinidad and Tobago which is 85 ft tall. Paritala mandal Kanchikacherla Andhra Pradesh 521180
