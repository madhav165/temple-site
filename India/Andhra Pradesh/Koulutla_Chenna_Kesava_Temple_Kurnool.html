Koulutla Chenna Kesava Temple Kurnool

Koulutla Chenna Kesava Temple is located in a reserved forest of Kappatralla 50 km from district Kurnool District headquarters. The great Vijayanagara King Sri Krishna Deva Raya constructed a temple complex for the deity. Along with Koulutla Chena Kesava Temple there are additional Shiva temples in this shrine.
