Nookalamma temple Visakhapatnam

Nookallamma or Nookambika is a local deity or Gramadevata popular in Andhra Pradesh State of India. The temple houses the goddess Nookambika. Appalaraju built this temple to Kakatambika a family goddess. She would later be called Nookambika or Nookalamma. On Kottha Amavasya a day before Ugadi thousands of people from throughout Andhra Pradesh come to the temple for worship
