Lakshmi Narasimha swamy temple Penna Ahobilam Andhra Pradesh

Lakshmi Narasimha swamy temple Penna Ahobilam Andhra Pradesh Temple was built on the footprint of Lord Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy measuring 5 feet 3 inches. Temple Timings 8.00 am to 11.00 am 5.30 pm to 8.30 pm. The Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy temple is a popular location for marriage ceremonies. A grand car festival is conducted annually in the month of April. Official Address Penna Ahobilam Rd Penna Ahobilam Andhra Pradesh 515812
