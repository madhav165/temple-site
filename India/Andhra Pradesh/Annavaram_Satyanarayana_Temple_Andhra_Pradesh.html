Annavaram Satyanarayana Temple Andhra Pradesh Sri Veera Venkata Satyanarayanaswamy Temple or Annavaram Temple is a Hindu-Vaishnavite temple situated at Annavaram town in East Godavari District of Andhra Pradesh state India.The temple is on a hillock named Ratnagiri and is dedicated to Veera Venkata Satyanarayana an incarnation of Lord Vishnu.
