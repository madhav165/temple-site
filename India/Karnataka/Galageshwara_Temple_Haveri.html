Galageshwara Temple Haveri

Galageshwara Temple also knowns as Galaganatha Temple and is located in a small village named Galaganath which was earlier known as Palluni. It was constructed during the rule of Chalukyas. It is a breathtaking Shiva Temple with sculptures and intricate carvings. The nearest airport is Hubli. The nearest railway station is at Haveri which is at a distance of 30 kms from Galaganath. The NH-4 will take you to Ranebennur taluka of Haveri. Haveri lies at a distance of 350 kms from Bangalore. From here you need to head towards Agadi road for 15 kms and then make a detour after Guttal. The village is only 15 kms away from here.
