Hoysaleshwara Temple Hassan

Halebidu temple is another great example of temple architecture the twin temples of Hoysaleshwara Kedareshwara built by Vishnuvardhana and Ballala-II at Halabidu. Sequences from Ramayana Mahabharatha Bhagavatha are depicted on the outer walls of these temples. The efficiency and experience of the sculpture who had carved these stories in stone is bewitching. Hassan does not have an airport and the nearest airport is Bangalore from where one can take flights to other major destinations in India and abroad. Hassan is connected by rail to Bangalore Mysore Hubli Shimoga and Mangalore. Halebeed is 17 Km from Belur and 30 Km from Hassan.
