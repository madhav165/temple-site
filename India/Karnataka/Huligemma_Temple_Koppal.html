Huligemma Temple Koppal

The Huligemma temple is a 13th century temple devoted to Goddess Huligemma. The nearest airports is Jindal Vijaynagar Airport 72.3Km and Hubli Airport 123.4Km . Nearest Railway stations are Hospet Munirabad Koppal. Two important National Highways NH50 NH67 and Four State Highways SH36 SH130 SH59 SH63 intersect at Koppal.
