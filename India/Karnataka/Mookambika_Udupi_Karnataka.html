Mookambika Udupi Karnataka

Mookambika Udupi Karnataka As per legends there lived a sage in ancient years named Kola Maharishi who was troubled by a demon. The demon was in penance to obtain boon from gods only for the sake of troubling more. Goddess Parvathi made the demon dumb meaning Mooka so he cant ask for boons. The demon was called as Mookasura meaning Dumb demon . The frustrated mute demon started creating even more problems to the sage and the people around. Upon the appealed request of the sage Goddess Parvathi descended in a powerful form and killed Mookasura. So the Goddess came to be hailed as Mookambika. The sage requested the Goddess to stay in that place and so did Goddess took her abode in the same place called Kollur. Official Address
