Sri Sri Narasingha Akhara Temple

Narsingh Akhara temple is another oldest temple located at Garikhana in Snhillong Meghalaya. Devotees celebrate the biggest annual festival- Rath Yatra along with Jagannath temple of Puri in Odisa every year with a great pomp and devotion.
