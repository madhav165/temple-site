Kalachakra Gompa Arunachal Pradesh

Kalachakra is a 500 years old monastery in Arunachal Pradesh which makes it quite a famous tourist attraction in the state. The monastery is situated in a village of the same name. It is a beautiful little monastery with mosaic work. Mostly Buddhist monks and pilgrims are seen visiting this sacred place. One has to reach Kalachakra Village by a cab and then has to walk all the way to the Gompa. Visit the nearby Dirang Village Sangti Valley and Hot Springs. The period between November and February is ideal to pay a visit. The mercury dips down and it becomes quite pleasant to walk around.
