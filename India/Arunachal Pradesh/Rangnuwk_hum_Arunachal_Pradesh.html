Rangnuwk hum Arunachal Pradesh Rang Nuwk Hum is a worship place for the Tangsa people of Northeast India and Myanmar. Rang means the God Nuwk means pray and Hum means a small house that contain a picture of the God Rangfrah.Rangfrah is believed to be an incarnation of the Hindu lord Shiva. Devotees go to a Rang Nuwk Hum everyday to give offerings and to pray which is followed by singing local bhajans. Official Address
