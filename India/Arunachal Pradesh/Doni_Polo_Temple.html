Doni Polo Temple

Started in the year 1989 by Talom Rukbo a revivalist religious movement based in Arunachal Pradesh Donyi Polo Temple is a center to worship sun and moon. It is one of the 400 centers of Arunachal Pradesh that propagates the ancient religion Donyi Poloism. To reach Donyi Polo Temple one can hire a private taxi or go by state tourism buses.
