Dadra and Nagar Haveli

Dadra and Nagar Haveli a union territory located in western India is home to several sacred temples that reflect the region s rich cultural heritage and offer visitors a chance to explore their spirituality. From the famous Bindrabin Temple to the serene Balaji Temple Dadra and Nagar Haveli is a must-visit destination for those seeking to connect with their spiritual side. The Bindrabin Temple located in Bindrabin village is one of the most famous temples. The temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is known for its serene atmosphere and stunning architecture. The temple s location in the midst of lush greenery adds to its tranquil ambiance. Another notable temple in Dadra and Nagar Haveli is the Balaji Temple located in Silvassa the capital city of the union territory. The temple is dedicated to Lord Venkateshwara also known as Lord Balaji and is considered one of the most important temples in the region. The temple s intricate carvings and stunning architecture make it a popular destination for tourists and devotees alike. The Vanganga Lake and Garden located in Silvassa is also home to several temples including the Swaminarayan Temple and the Sai Baba Temple. These temples provide visitors with a peaceful retreat and a chance to connect with their spiritual side. Dadra and Nagar Haveli is also home to several other significant temples including the Church of Our Lady of Piety and the Shree Swaminarayan Temple. Each temple offers visitors a unique opportunity to explore the region s cultural heritage and connect with their spirituality.
