Shiva Temple Ahmednagar

Temple of Shiva on either side of the stream There are two temples of Shiva located at a distance of nearly 1.5 km from Parner at the meeting of two small streams and they are locally known as Sangameshvara and Trimbakeshvara temple.It is datable to 12th century AD.
