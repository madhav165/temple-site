Bhadra Maruti Temple Aurangabad

Bhadra Maruti Temple Khultabad is a temple dedicated to the Hindu deity Hanuman located at Khuldabad near Aurangabad Maharashtra. The temple is located four kilometers from the Ellora Caves At this temple the idol of Hanuman is portrayed in a reclining or sleeping posture.It is one of only three places where Hanuman is represented in a sleeping posture. The second noted place is a temple on the banks of Ganga at sangam in Allahabad and Uttar Pradesh and the third is at Jam Sawali Madhya Pradesh The Bhadra Maruti Temple is considered one of the tourist attractions near Aurangabad and people gather in lakhs during auspicious occasions like Hanuman Jayanti and Ram Navami Khuldabad Rd Khuldabad Maharashtra 431101 02437 241 458
