Jivdani Mata Palghar

Jivdani Mata is a Hindu Goddess. The main temple of the goddess is situated atop a hill in Virar Maharashtra India. The temple is on the hill almost 1500 ft from the sea-level. The Goddess rests in a temple situated about 1250 steps above the ground on a hill that forms a part of the Satpura Range in Virar a northern Mumbai suburb about 60 km away from Mumbai
