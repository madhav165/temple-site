Koleshwar Ratnagiri

Shri Dev Koleshwar is an ancient Shiva Temple located at Kolthare a village from Dapoli Taluka of Ratnagiri District from Maharashtra state in India. A cave temple of Chankai devi is located at about 6 km at Dabhol. Ratnagiri is connected by road to Mumbai Madgaon Mangalore Udupi Cochin Karwar by NH17 and these places are connected by direct flights via Delhi and Jaipur.
