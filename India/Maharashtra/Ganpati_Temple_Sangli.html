Ganpati Temple Sangli

The Lord Ganapati Temple is situated in the city of Tasgaon within the Sangli district in the Indian state of Maharashtra. Most of the Ganapati idols have a left-sided trunk while this temple s idol s trunk bends towards the right.The Ganapati idol with the trunk curved towards the right is said to be active jagrut . This Ganapati is regarded as a living idol ready to bless communities with good luck wisdom prosperity and happiness. The idol is embellished with solid gold weighing 125 kilograms 276 lb .
