Babulnath Mumbai

Babulnath is an ancient Shiva temple in Mumbai India. Situated on a small hillock near Girgaum Chowpatty it is one of oldest temples in the city Shiva in the form of the Lord of the Babul tree is the main deity in this temple. The faithful climb up to the temple and obtain Darshan of the shivling and obtain blessings of the Lord. It is also possible to take an elevator up to the temple. The temples is visited by lakhs of devotees on annual Mahashivratri festival You can reach this place by taxi cab from Charni Road Station West .Its just adjacent to Famous Girgaon Girgaum Chowpatty and back side of Wilson College.
