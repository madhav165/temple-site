Adasa Ganesha Temple Nagpur

Adasa Ganesha temple located 45 km away from the city of Nagpur is situated on a hillock. It s an ancient temple to Lord Ganesha and is considered to be 4 000 years old. The temple is considered one of the eight Ashta-Vinayaks in Vidarbha. The presiding deity is an idol of Ganesha standing 12 feet tall and 7 feet wide and is made of a monolithic stone. This idol is also believed by some to have evolved on its own. During Pausha in winters a major fair is organized in the village and many pilgrims flock to the place during that time. Its 40 Km from the city and a car can reach very close say only around a 100 meters away post which there are about 40 steps to climb to the temple.
