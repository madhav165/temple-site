Shri Dev Rameshwar Temple Sindhudurg

Shri Dev Rameshwar Temple is located in village Rameshwar Wadi in Devgad taluka of Sindhudurg District Maharashtra India. This temple is dedicated to the Hindu deity Lord Shiva. It is a historic temple built in 16th century. Its chief interest is the approach about 250 yards long cut through rock fifty feet deep. The idol a four-armed figure seated on a bull is of solid silver said to weigh a hundred kilograms and is in good condition at present. Talgoan Maharashtra
