Kukdeshwar Temple Pune

Kukdeshwar Temple is located in Pune District of Maharashtra India.It is about 15 km west of Junnar and lies on the banks of Kukdi River. It is a Shiv temple noted for its beautiful sculptures and carvings. The roof of this temple is in a dilapidated state. Chavand fort also known as Prasannagad is approximately 3 km from Kukdeshwar temple. Nearest Town is Junnar. Kukdeshwar Temple is located in the Pur village on the bank of Kukdi River at a distance of 15 Km from Junnar on its western side in the Pune district ofMaharashtra.
