Bhavani Osmanabad

Bhavani also known as Tulaja Turaja Tvarita Amb? and Jagadamb? is an avatar of the Hindu goddess Parvati . She is a form of Durga who is worshiped in Maharashtra and also by Gurjars of North Gujarat Northern Karnataka Western Rajasthan and Punjab.Bhavani translates to giver of life meaning the power of nature or the source of creative energy. She is considered to be a mother who provides to her devotees and also plays the role of dispensing justice by killing Asuras. Lord Shiva as Bhava Goddess Parvati as Bhavani. This temple is located in Tuljapur Maharashtra. The nearest airport to Tuljapur is Chikkalthana Airport and the nearest rail head is at Osmanabad. You can easily get regular buses to Tuljapur from other major cities of the country.
