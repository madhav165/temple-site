The History of Parli Vaijnath Temple says that Rani Ahilyabhai renovated the Parli Vaijnath Temple in the 1700s. Two very popular legends are associated with this temple. One of the legends talks about the Amrit and the other talks about the demon King Ravana and his quest to own Shiva. Nearest Airport is Aurangabad which is 225 km away Railway Station at Parli. Regular State Transport Buses are available from any Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation Bus Stand.
