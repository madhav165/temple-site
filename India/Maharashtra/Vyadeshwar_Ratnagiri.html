Vyadeshwar Ratnagiri

Shri Dev Vyadeshwar is a Hindu temple dedicated to the God Shiva. It is located in Guhagar Ratnagiri District of the Indian state of Maharashtra State.It is considered the Kuladevata Clan God of many Chitpavan families from the Konkan region. According to Hindu mythology sage Parashurama created the land of Konkan by shooting his arrow into the sea and commanding the Sea God to retract the waters to the point where his arrow landed.
