Deo Surya Mandir Aurangabad

Deo Surya Mandir English Name Deo Sun Temple is a Hindu temple in Bihar India. The temple is a Sun shrine dedicated to Lord Sun for Chhath Puja. The temple is located in Deo Town in the Indian State of Bihar. The Temple is unique as it faces west the setting sun not the usual rising sun. This temple is reported to be considered very auspicious to visit on the festival of Chatth puja Adra nakshatra every Sunday. Deo Bihar 824202
