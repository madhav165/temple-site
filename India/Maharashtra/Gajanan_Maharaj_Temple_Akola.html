Gajanan Maharaj Temple Akola

Shri Sant Gajanan Maharaj is known to most of the people in Maharashtra. He stayed most of his life at Shegaon near Akola district in Maharashtra and has taken Samadhi there. Therefore Shegaon has got a special significance for Maharaj and his devotees. Shegaon hosts a magnificent temple. His devotees have constructed many Gajanan Maharaj Temples in different parts of Maharashtra India. A small temple has also been constructed at Kanhor Kulgaon Badlapur Dist. Thane.
