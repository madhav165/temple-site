Mudikkode Shiva Temple Thrissur Kerala

Pananchery Mudikkode Shiva Temple is an ancient Hindu temple dedicated to Shiva at Pananchery of Thrissur District in Kerala statein India. The presiding deity of the temple is Lord Shiva located in main Sanctum Sanctorum facing West. According to folklore sage Parashurama has installed the idol. The temple is a part of the 108 famous Shiva temples in Kerala
