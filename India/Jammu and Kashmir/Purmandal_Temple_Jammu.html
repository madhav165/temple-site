Purmandal Temple Jammu

Purmandal Temple often referred as Chhota Kashi is a temple complex dedicated to Lord Shiva and his consort Parvati. At a distance of about 30 km from Jammu city it is located on the bank of River Devak. There are multi shiv lingams at the temple and most of the lingam have originated naturally called in local dialect as Aap Shambhu .
