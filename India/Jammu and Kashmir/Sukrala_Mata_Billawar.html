Sukrala Mata Billawar

The famous shrine of Sukrala Mata is located in Billawar in Kathua district at a height of 3500 feet from sea level. According to local belief the temple is abode of goddess Mal Devi re-incarnation of Sharda Devi. It is believed to have been built by Madho Singh exiled prince of Chamba of Himachal Pradesh. Thousands of devotees throng the shrine during Navratras The most famous shrine in Billawar is Sukrala Devi Shrine at a height of 3500 ft on a hillock amidst sylvan surroundings at a distance of 10 km fromBillawar and about 75 km from Kathua.
