Mangalam Devi Temple Badgam

Mangalam Devi Temple is a revered centre of Idukki district located at a distance of 15 km from Thekkady in Kerala. It is perched on a hill at an altitude of 1337 above the sea level. The auspicious occasion of Chitra Pournami is celebrated with great enthusiasm at the shrine.
