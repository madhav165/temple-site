Sarvamangala Temple Korba

Sarvamangala is one of the famous temples in the Korba district. The Goddess of this temple is Durga. This temple was built by the ancestors of Rajeshwar Dayal one of the Zamindar in Korba. The temple is surrounded by Trilokinath Mandir Kali Mandir and Jyoti Kalash Bhavan. There is also a cave which goes under the river and comes out on the other side. Rani Dhanraj Kunwar Devi was used to this cave for her daily visit to the temple. Swami Vivekanand Airport Raipur is the nearest airport. 200 KM Korba Railway station 5 KM. Bus stand Korba 5 KM.
