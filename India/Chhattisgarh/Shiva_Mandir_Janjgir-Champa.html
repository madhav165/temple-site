Shiva Mandir Janjgir-Champa

The Lord Shiva Mandir is situated on the bank of river Hasdeo which is also known as Kaleshwarnath Mandir. A 10 days fate and fair is being organized here during the festival of Maha Shivaratri and Rang Panchami. Every year on RangPanchami the marriage procession Lord Shiva is being organized in which a group Naga Saints also takes part. Raipur Chhattisgarh is nearest Airport Janjgir-Naila is nearest Railway station 10 K.M. TOWARDS EAST from Janjgir
