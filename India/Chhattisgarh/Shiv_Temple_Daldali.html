Shiv Temple Daldali

About 10 km. East side of Mahasamund there is a place called Daldali there is an old Shiva temple ghaudhara.The water flows constantly from the ghaudhara. The village Daldali s northeast side is covered by beautiful forest. During the months of Sravan and Poush devotees in great numbers offer prayers here. During FULLMOON of Poush Mela is celebrated here. Tourists also visit this place. The nearest airport is at Raipur. The nearest railhead is at Mahasamund. The nearest bus stand is at Mahasamund.
