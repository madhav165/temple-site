Shri Bhairav Nath Ji Ratanpur

Adi Shakti Mahamaya Kaumari as Shaktipeeth sits in the legendary city of Ratanpur in Chhattisgarh the same is the temple at the entrance of her protector Bhairav Nath. This temple is famous from the Siddha Tantra Peeth Sri Kal Bhairav Temple. It is on the right hand side in Bilaspur Ratanpur road. Thousands of devotees come to see Bhairav and fulfill the desired wish. The divine and magnificent temple of Mahamaya Devi about 1000 years old is worth visiting. It was built by King Ratnadeva I in the 11th century.
