Laxmneshwar Temple Janjgir-Champa

According Hindu mythology and as per the local story a Shiv Temple was built here by younger brother of Bhagwan Ram Laxman. That is why the temple is known as Laxmneshwar Temple. It is also a great place for Hindu pilgrims. Raipur Chhattisgarh is nearest Airport Janjgir-Naila is nearest Railway station. 20 K.M. FROM TAHSIL HEAD QUARTER PAMGARH TOWARDS SOUTH.
