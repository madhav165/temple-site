Ganga Maiya Durg

Temple Name Ganga Maiya is a holy place situated in Jhalmala a place near Balod in Durg District. During the Hindu festival of Navratri this place is very crowded. Many worshipers of Maa Durga come here to every year to celebrate the Navratri. Navratri is celebrated twice a year i.e. in the Hindu month of Chaitra and Kunwar. During these nine days devotes keep fasting and thousands of people visit this place barefoot and they come by walking from long distance. Official Address
