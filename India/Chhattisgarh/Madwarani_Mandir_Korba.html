Madwarani Mandir Korba

Madwarani Mandir is situated on the Korba-Champa Road at a distance 22 KM from district headquarter.There is a temple of Godess Madwarani at the top of the hill. The legend behind this temple is that at the season of Nawaratri jowar was growing under Kalmi trees near the temple. During the Nawratri season September. October of each year there is fair celebrated by the local people. Swami Vivekanand Airport is the nearest airport. 200 KM Korba Railway station 30KM. And Champa Railway station 35 KM.
