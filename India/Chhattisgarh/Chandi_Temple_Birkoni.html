Chandi Temple Birkoni

The Chandi temple is in village Birkoni on N.H.6 about 10 km. north of Mahasamund. The construction of the temple is in progress. During Navarathri of Chaitr Kuwar the devotees offer the light igniting. The nearest airport is at Raipur. The nearest railhead is at Mahasamund. The nearest bus stand is at Mahasamund.
