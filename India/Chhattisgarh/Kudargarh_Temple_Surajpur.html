Kudargarh Temple Surajpur

District of the state of Chhattisgarh in India. It is 25 km from district headquarters of Surajpur connected by an all weather road. The best time to visit is during Chaitra Navaratra in the month of April . Raipur Airport is the nearest airport. Surajpur railway station is nearest from tourist place Surajpur bus-stop is nearest from tourist place
