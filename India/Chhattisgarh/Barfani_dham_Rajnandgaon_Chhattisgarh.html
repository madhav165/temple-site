Barfani dham Rajnandgaon Chhattisgarh

Barfani dham Rajnandgaon Chhattisgarh Barfani dham is a temple in the town of Rajnandgaon in the Indian state of Chhattisgarh.. It is 40 kilometres 25 mi from Durg towards Nagpur. A large Shiva Linga can be seen at the top of the temple while a large Nandi statue stands in front of it The temple is constructed in three levels. The bottom layer is the shrine of Pathal bhairavi the second is the Navadurga or Tripura Sundari shrine and the upper level is of Shiv.
